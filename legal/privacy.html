Customer Telemetry Encryption

1. Data Logging
All customer telemetry collected during purchase transactions (including names, email addresses, and shipping routing coordinates) is encrypted and stored locally in system databases. No telemetry is transmitted to third-party advertising networks.

2. Transaction Safety
Credit card indices and CVV secure keys are parsed locally to authorize simulated checkouts and are not retained in system storage nodes after authorization logs are processed.

3. Cookie Nodes
We utilize local storage mechanisms (such as localStorage) solely to store your active workspace cart configuration and preferences.
